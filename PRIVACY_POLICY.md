# Privacy Policy

**List & Shop**

*Last updated: April 2, 2026*

## Overview

List & Shop is a fully offline shopping list app. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to be transparent: **this app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

## Data Collection

This app does **not** collect any data. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No cookies or similar technologies
- No third-party advertising or analytics SDKs
- No network connections — the app works entirely offline

## Data Storage

All shopping list data (lists, items, sections) is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
